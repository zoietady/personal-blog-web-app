My Hometown:Hawthorn

About

Though I am orginally from Manila, Philippines; I've moved to Hawthorn to pursue my studies in Swinburne University. Eversince I got here, I made myself accustomed to it and embraced it as my new home.

Hawthorn is a gorgeous small town just a 20 min train ride away from downtown Melbourne. It has a ton of amazing parks every 2 blocks or so, filled with amazing food hubs, and 2 story establishments that sport the old town look.

What's in Hawthorn?

Hawthorn is a pretty layed back town, but it doesn't mean it's no fun. As mention there are a lot of awesome retaurants which will bring out the foodie in anyone. There's also a ton of small boutique coffee shops that are home to the best beans I've tasted.

There's no shortage of entertainment as well, since there are a lot of areas for sports from swimming, to tennis, to basketball, to even swimming, and most importantly footsie. Classy bars with live music with all the genre's that will surely make each sip amazing are present every 2 blocks or so; and if your night is still not complete, the lido cinema is always just a few minutes aways.
